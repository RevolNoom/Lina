<Show matrices>
		show [--all | Matrix Name | Expression]
	+) If given no argument, Show lists all available matrices with their respective size.
	+) Draw the matrix you want by listing its name.
	+) "show --all" draws all the matrices you have ever created.
	+) You can choose to write out the result of an expression
		E.g.:
			show ABC+DEF
			show ABC*DEF
		For more information, please refer to "help expression"
