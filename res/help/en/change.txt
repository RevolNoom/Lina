<Change the value of Matrix>
		change <Matrix Names> [Rows x columns | Expression]
	+) Enter new values for the matrix row by row:
		change ABC
	+) Change the size of the matrix to 10x10 and enter new elements:
		change ABC 10x10
	+) Assign new value to your matrix:
		change ABC=ABC+DEF
		change ABC=ABC*(DEF+ABC)
	For more information about Expression, please refer to "help expression"
