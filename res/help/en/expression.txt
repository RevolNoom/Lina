<Writing Expressions>
	<Matrix Name 1> [operator] <Matrix Name 2> [...] 
	+) Supported operators: + (sum), - (subtraction), * (multiplication), ^ (power).
	+) Power operation ^ takes a fraction or matrix as base, and raise it to the power of an integer.
		(NOTE: You must wrap the power part inside parentheses)
		ABC^(2), ABC^(-1), ABC^(10)
	+) You can use parentheses to group expressions together.
		E.g.: (ABC+DEF)*(DEF-ABC)
	+) Future update might include functions to work with
		(Inverse Matrix, Determinant, ...)
